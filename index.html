Magic Souls

Magic Souls is a third-person action game that was developed in Unity as a team project for my Computer Graphics course. In the game, you control a wizard who must restore peace to his valley. After the course ended, my team and I decided to further polish it and publish it on itch.io. As part of the team, I was responsible for implementing the shop system, designing the UI, and developing the enemy AI. Additionally, I composed the game's music using FL Studio and integrated it with FMOD.

VR Serious Video Game

This is my dissertation project, which involved designing and developing a virtual reality serious video game using Unreal Engine 5 to teach structural engineering concepts. The game was tested by a group of 25 civil engineering students from the university, and the results demonstrated the effectiveness of using VR serious video games to teach or reinforce structural engineering concepts.

Click here to see the project!

Pong Proximal Policy Optimization Self-Play Agent using Unity ML-Agents

This project involved comparing the effectiveness of using Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO) alone versus using it with competitive self-play to create an agent that can play Pong, using Unity ML-Agents. I completed this project as the final assignment for my Intelligent Systems course.

Click here to see the project!

GPU Flocking

This project is a comparison of flocking AI that runs on the CPU vs one that runs on the GPU. This was developed using Unity as the final project of my Programming Languages course.

Click here to see the project!

Origins

Origins is a Java-based action game developed by my team for the final project of our Video Game Project course. The game features a native Aztec protagonist during the first Viceroyalty of New Spain in 1600 and requires players to survive timed rounds by eliminating enemy colonizers. As a team member, I was responsible for developing the enemy AI and the core gameplay loop.
